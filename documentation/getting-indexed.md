# Getting Your Website Indexed by Google

If your website doesn't appear in Google search results, it likely hasn't been indexed yet. Here's how to fix it.

## Quick Steps to Get Indexed

### 1. Submit to Google Search Console (Most Important)

1. **Go to Google Search Console**: https://search.google.com/search-console
2. **Add Property**: Click "Add Property" → Enter your website URL: `https://exotu-website.vercel.app`
3. **Verify Ownership**: Choose one of these methods:
   - **HTML tag** (easiest): Copy the meta tag Google provides
   - **HTML file upload**: Download and upload to your site
   - **DNS record**: Add a TXT record to your domain
   - **Google Analytics**: If you have GA set up
4. **Submit Sitemap**: Once verified:
   - Go to "Sitemaps" in the left menu
   - Enter: `https://exotu-website.vercel.app/sitemap.xml`
   - Click "Submit"
5. **Request Indexing**: 
   - Go to "URL Inspection" tool
   - Enter your homepage URL: `https://exotu-website.vercel.app/`
   - Click "Request Indexing"

### 2. Verify Your Site is Accessible

Test these URLs to make sure they work:
- Homepage: https://exotu-website.vercel.app/
- Sitemap: https://exotu-website.vercel.app/sitemap.xml
- Robots.txt: https://exotu-website.vercel.app/robots.txt

### 3. Check if Already Indexed

Search Google for: `site:exotu-website.vercel.app`

If you see results, your site is indexed! If not, continue with the steps below.

## Why LinkedIn/Instagram Show Up First

- **More Authority**: LinkedIn and Instagram have high domain authority
- **Already Indexed**: They've been around longer and are well-established
- **More Backlinks**: Social media platforms get linked from many places
- **Regular Updates**: Social media content is updated frequently

## Troubleshooting

### Site Still Not Showing After 2 Weeks

1. **Check Search Console**: Look for errors or warnings
2. **Verify robots.txt**: Make sure it's not blocking crawlers
3. **Check sitemap**: Ensure it's accessible and valid
4. **Test mobile-friendliness**: Use Google's Mobile-Friendly Test
5. **Check page speed**: Use PageSpeed Insights

### Common Issues

- **robots.txt blocking**: Check that `Allow: /` is present
- **No sitemap**: Ensure sitemap.xml is accessible
- **Slow loading**: Optimize images and code
- **Mobile issues**: Fix responsive design problems
